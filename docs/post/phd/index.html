What is my PhD?

@Vicky Butt · Jun 15, 2017 · 1 min read

Realised I haven’t said much on the PhD front. Rotations are now over, and I’m currently drafting the PhD project. What I can be sure of is it contains many ‘omes’ and ‘omics’…

Here’s a sneak peak at what I’ll be looking at:

1) Analysis of metagenomic data of microbes in the oral cavity. Basically all the microbes’ DNA in a mouth..

2) Mobilome (funnier said aloud). Lots of mobile genetic elements, the genes that move across microbes:

3) Resistome. Lots of anti-microbial & antibiotic resistance in bacteria (and fungi!)

4) Maybe some genome-scale metabolic models, which are all the metabolic reactions in a microbial cell. Here’s an example from Goyel et al.

It’s ambitious.. time for some more refining!
